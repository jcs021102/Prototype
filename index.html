Overview

Bank of Valletta Plc (BOV) is a provider of banking and other related financial solutions. It offers personal banking, business banking, and wealth management services. BOV provides loans and deposits solutions, prepaid, credit and debit cards; and investment funds. It provides loans for home, motor, education, personal requirements. The bank also offers insurance plans for life and retirement. It also offers financial planning and corporate finance solutions.
